Elementary Analysis

Time and place:

10:30am–11:20am MWF OLDH 208. We will also have a zoom link in case you are temporarily unable to join in person. Everyone is expected to participate in person as circumstances permit, and online otherwise. Class time will involve a combination of lecture and socially-distanced groupwork. If you intend to attend class online only, you should transfer to a webinar section.

Textbook:

Understanding Real Analysis, by Paul Zorn, second edition.

Course content:

In this class, we will explore the real numbers, and understand precisely what makes calculus work. In this pursuit, we will develop our proof-writing techniques and our ability to pose and work with definitions. The course material breaks into four main topics:
  • Sets, proofs, and the real numbers
  • Sequences and series
  • Limits and continuity
  • Derivatives
