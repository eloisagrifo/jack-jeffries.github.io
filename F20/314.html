Final exam

The final exam will take place at the University scheduled time, listed on the syllabus, online on Canvas. You must submit your exam by the end of the two hour period, so I recommend that you be prepared to start promptly on time. The exam will include multiple questions for which you need to show your work, so be prepared with a piece of paper or whatever else you wish to use, and save some extra time at the end for submitting these questions. As with the quizzes, you may use your own prepared notes and your textbook, but you may NOT discuss with any other students, use any online resources or RREF calculators, etc. A calculator for adding, subtracting, and multiplying numbers is OK. If you have any questions about these technical aspects of the exam, please let me know.
